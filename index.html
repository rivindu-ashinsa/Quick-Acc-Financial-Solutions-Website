About Us

Quick Acc Financial Solutions is a premier audit and accounting firm dedicated to providing comprehensive financial services to businesses across various industries. With a commitment to excellence and precision, we have established ourselves as a trusted partner for organizations seeking reliable financial management and advisory services.

Our firm specializes in delivering end-to-end accounting solutions, from meticulous bookkeeping and financial reporting to strategic tax planning and regulatory compliance. We understand that every business has unique financial needs, and our tailored approach ensures that we meet and exceed client expectations at every turn.

Under the experienced leadership of Samantha Sanjaya Handuwala, our team combines deep technical expertise with practical business acumen. We pride ourselves on maintaining the highest standards of professional integrity, confidentiality, and ethical conduct. Our client-centric philosophy drives us to build long-lasting relationships based on trust, transparency, and exceptional service delivery.

Whether you are a startup navigating your first financial year or an established enterprise seeking to optimize your financial operations, Quick Acc Financial Solutions provides the expertise and support you need to achieve your business objectives with confidence.

Our Services

Bookkeeping & Accounting

  • Extracting information on transactions from relevant documents and maintaining primary books of accounts
  • Arranging payment vouchers with proper documentation
  • Maintaining ledger accounts and effecting journal entries where necessary
  • Maintaining customer and supplier ledgers with complete accuracy
  • Performing comprehensive bookkeeping functions for your company

Financial Reporting & Reconciliation

  • Preparation of monthly bank reconciliation statements
  • Providing schedules for payables at each month end
  • Reconciliation of related party current accounts
  • Providing schedules for suspense accounts
  • Reconciliation of returned cheques
  • Preparation of statements of Income, Balance Sheet, and Changes in Equity with related notes on a monthly basis
  • Preparation of outlet-wise trading accounts on a monthly basis
  • Providing relevant management reports tailored to your needs

Compliance & Regulatory Services

  • Ensuring proper compliance with various applicable tax laws
  • Ensuring proper compliance with other laws, rules, and regulations
  • Ensuring proper compliance with Management Information Systems, including generation of monthly management accounts

System Implementation & Advisory

  • Supervision and coordination of work for existing staff at the finance division
  • Designing and implementing proper systems and controls to ensure operational efficiency
  • Implementation of effective budgetary control systems
  • Assistance in completing the development of software packages in an expedited manner
